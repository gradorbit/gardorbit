Purpose

In today’s ultra-competitive and specialized job market, grads need the continuous support and empathy of mentors and peers to achieve their career aspirations, develop a peace of mind during the job hunt, and land the right first job. To address this vital need, Grad Orbit offers personalized mentoring to jumpstart a grad’s career.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empathize with, energize, and empower grads to start their career.

Our Vision

Our vision is to foster a community of industry professionals dedicated to grad success.

Our Values

  • Integrity & Respect
  • Trust in the mentor
  • Empathy for the grad
  • Pay it Forward attitude
  • Accountability for outcome
The purpose is to mentor grads into a stable career orbit. While our focal point is the grad, our goal is for all parties to benefit from this collaborative effort: grads, mentors, and hiring companies. We want grads to be proactive, networked, and confidently prepared when looking for their first job. During this critical phase of their professional career, we expect that grads experience a memorable journey and join an organization that is a good match. Through our initiative, employers will have access to grads who are more professionally savvy, empathetic, and industry aware than their peers.

—Aruna Nadesan founder of Grad Orbit
