Finding Your First Job: How College Grads Benefit from Mentoring

In today’s ultra-competitive and specialized job market, grads need the continuous support and empathy of mentors and peers to achieve their career aspirations, develop a peace of mind during the job hunt, and land the right first job. To address this vital need, Grad Orbit offers personalized mentoring to jumpstart a grad’s career.

Introducing GOprofile

For each GOgrad, Grad Orbit creates an individualized three-dimensional profile that captures a grad’s academic background, work experience, and persona. This profile establishes and promotes a grad’s personal brand.

GOprofile benefits three parties: the grad, the mentor, and the employer. By creating this profile, a grad is able to articulate their unique worth. Grad Orbit also expects that the grad will gain confidence after visualizing their comprehensive background. With the data provided by grads during the GOprofile information collecting process, a mentor can better direct a grad toward suitable job opportunities. Plus, the profile helps the mentor identify a grad’s skills and strengths. In turn, the mentor can coach a grad on how to best use and express these abilities during the interview process and beyond. Lastly, the profile gives the employer a better picture of the grad.

Using information from a grad’s initial resume, GOprofile information collection sheet and the chat logs between mentor and mentee, Grad Orbit crafts an authentic narrative that delves into a grad’s unique personality and skill sets. We believe that this form of storytelling heightens a grad’s self-assurance by elucidating their distinct value to the job market.

GOprofile Creation Approach

A grad’s narrative is divided into the same three categories contained in the GOprofile: Academic Achievements, Work Experience, and Persona. This narrative expands on the most noteworthy data from the GOprofile information collection sheet, and it does so in a logical and easy-to-read manner. Written in the third-person point of view, each GOprofile narrative maintains a professional tone while illustrating a grad’s distinctive background.

Introduction

This section provides a brief introduction of the Grad and his/her career aspiration.

Academic Achievements

This section often begins with a broad overview of a grad’s academic history, noting an individual’s degree(s), alma mater, specialization, and thesis. The narrative then recounts significant academic projects, events, research, and competitions from a grad’s collegiate life.

Work Experience

This section highlights a grad’s most impressive work experiences. The narrative expounds on a grad’s key roles and duties at jobs, internships, and/or co-op programs. Moreover, these passages emphasize the professional skills that a grad applied and developed while working. This section also mentions a grad’s most successful work projects.

Persona

This section delves into a grad’s core identity, revealing their strengths, characteristics, values, aspirations, and more. To gain a broader picture of a grad, the narrative demonstrates how a grad’s unique disposition impacts their academic and career decisions and accomplishments.

Contact Her/Him

As an addendum, this section contains a grad’s contact info (email, phone number, etc.), which is for the benefit of interested employers.
